FAQs

Registration Related

How do I Register?
 You can register by clicking on the "Sign Up" link at the top right corner of the homepage. Please provide the information in the form that appears. You can review the terms and conditions, provide your payment mode details and submit the registration information.

Are there any charges for registration?
 No. Registration on my-dukaan.com is absolutely free.

Can I have multiple registration?
 Each contact phone number and email address can only be associated with one my-dukaan account.

Do I have to necessarily register for shopping?
 You can surf and add products to the cart without registration but only registered shoppers will be able to checkout and place orders. Registered members have to be logged in at the time of checking out the cart, they will be prompted to do so if they are not logged in.

Account Related

What is My Account?
 My Account is the section you reach after you log in at my-dukaan.com. My Account allows you to track your active orders, credit note details as well as see your order history and update your contact details.

How do I reset my password?
 You need to enter your mobile number or email address on the Login page and click on forgot password. An email with a reset password will be sent to your registered mobile number and email address. With this, you can change your password. In case of any further issues please contact our customer support team.

What is delivery confirmation PIN, where I can find it & can I edit it?
 Delivery confirmation PIN is a unique 4 digit number tied to every customer which enables you to give us service ratings at the time of order delivery.
